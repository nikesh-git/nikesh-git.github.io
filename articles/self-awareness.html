Self Awareness kicked out of window(screen)....

I am 90s kid. That means i have witnessed the transition of society towards digitization and attention economy.
Its fairly accepted fact that a huge proportion of population is addicted for byte size content. Even the fingers seem to have developed muscle memory of mindless scrolling.
Its not uncommon to witness people scrolling with one hand while eating with the other. And some can't even poop without consuming content.

Everyones aware of the effects of doom-scrolling. But as it has been my approach for life is to focus more on good over garbage.
Through this writing i want to focus on the acts of self-awareness.
Que.What it was like before social media?
Ans. Contemlation...lots of contemplation...about self
People would have to look for escapes to stop thinking and since it used to take work to find escapes, it was naturally throtled introducing a sweet and healthy balance.
You would have to socialize, get out of your house, play etc. to escape.

In present, we all have a palm size escape in our pockets loaded with apps that grab your brain by the throat and strangle it to sleep.

It feels amazingly stable to have some self-awareness and able to devote time and mental energy contemplating about oneself.
